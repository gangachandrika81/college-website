BV Raju Institute Of Tecnology (BVRIT)

BVRIT (B.V. Raju Institute of Technology), located near Hyderabad in Narsapur, is a well-regarded engineering college established by the Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES) in 1997, known for its focus on practical learning, robust R&D, placements, and empowering women engineers through its specialized women's college. Affiliated with JNTUH, it holds NAAC 'A' Grade, offers numerous B.Tech/PG programs, boasts modern labs, and sees strong placements, with top packages reaching significant figures.

Key Highlights:

  • Establishment: 1997, under SVES.
  • Location: Narsapur, Greater Hyderabad area, on a large 110-acre campus.
  • Affiliation/Accreditation: JNTUH, UGC Autonomous, NBA, NAAC Grade A.
  • Academics: Offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA programs with specializations in CSE, Data Sciences, VLSI, etc., emphasizing R&D and industry integration.
