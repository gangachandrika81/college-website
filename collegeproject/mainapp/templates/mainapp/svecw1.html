Sri Vishnu Engineering College for Women (SVECW)

SVECW (Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women) in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, is a leading engineering institution established in 2001 for women, focusing on rural empowerment through quality technical education (B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, Ph.D.). Known for strong placements, innovative labs, and green campus initiatives, it offers comprehensive facilities like hostels, libraries, sports, and medical support, aiming for academic excellence and self-reliance among its students, affiliated with JNTUK and accredited by NAAC/NBA.

Key Aspects:

  • Focus: Exclusively for women, with a strong emphasis on empowering rural students.
  • Location: Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, on a 100-acre green campus.
  • Courses: B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, Ph.D. in various streams.
  • Affiliation & Accreditation: Affiliated with JNTUK, Kakinada; accredited by NAAC (A+) and NBA.
  • Placements: Consistently high placement rates, with students in major companies like Amazon, Flipkart, Honda.
  • Infrastructure: Well-equipped labs (e.g., TI DSP Lab, Assistive Tech Lab), libraries, hostels, sports complexes, and eco-friendly practices (solar, rainwater harvesting).
  • Vision: To transform society through women's education, community empowerment, and environmental protection.

Vision & Mission:

To transform society through women's empowerment, community upliftment, and environmental care, achieving academic excellence with innovative learning and instilling strong values.

In essence, SVECW stands out as a leading women's engineering college in India, known for its excellent academic environment, strong technical focus, and successful placement records, especially for rural women.
